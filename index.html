Bonjour, y'all! Ahlan wa sahlan.

We are Kika — an online artisanal bakery reimagining Egyptian tradition with a modern boutique elegance. Every morning, we craft small batches of heritage breads, sweet pastries, and savory delights using time-honored techniques and the finest Egyptian grains.

From our signature Feteer Meshaltet to slow-fermented sourdough breads made with ancient heritage grains, every item is crafted for a New Haute Egyptian-twist. We are strictly online, focusing all our artistry on delivering the perfect crunch to your doorstep across Cairo. No reservations or dress code required—just your love for fine dough.
