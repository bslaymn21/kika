About Lapis

Bonjour, y'all! Or rather, ahlan wa sahlan. We are the little online storefront redefining Washington Avenue's unassuming charm with a Cairo heartbeat. Just a few steps away from tradition, Lapis is where Egyptian heritage meets modern boutique elegance.

From our signature Feteer Meshaltet to sourdough breads made with ancient heritage grains, every item is crafted for a New Haute Egyptian-twist. We are strictly online, focusing all our artistry on delivering the perfect crunch to your doorstep across Cairo. No reservations or dress code required—just your love for fine dough.
